Google, the parent company that is now known as Alphabet, is an amazing application. More than that, it is a group of apps that is made up of many different apps. Through Google, Alphabet can be a search engine. They have their own e-mail servers. They have their own office applications. Alphabet owns YouTube, a video sharing website that allows users to create content and share it. YouTube offers a paid subscription service that eliminates advertisements and allows you to download videos. The YouTube Music application allows you to listen to videos without having to render them.
